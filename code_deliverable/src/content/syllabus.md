# **TECHIE 1121 Ethical Vibe Coding (2026SU)**

#### *Good Code, Good Vibes — Building Ethical Apps with AI*

#### \[Summer 2026\]

**Instructor(s):** Hauke Sandhaus, Jonathan Segal, Wendy Ju

**Credits and Grading: EX:** 1, Letter Grade-Only

**Course Description:** These days, you can build apps with AI tools with the push of a button... but should you? In this three-week course, you will learn to "vibe code" real applications in an ethical manner. We will explore bias in AI systems, test the limits and vulnerabilities of these tools, and perform hands-on design while centering human values. No programming experience is required; just bring your laptop, your questions about what "AI for good" really means, and your appetite for creative problem-solving. Join us to learn not only how to build applications quickly, but also how to build them right.

**Course Objectives/Learning Outcomes:**

* Understand how AI-assisted coding tools work, including their strengths, limitations, and common failures.  
* Identify and analyze bias and risks in AI systems and generated outputs.  
* Design and build simple applications using AI tools.  
* Evaluate AI-generated code for accuracy and reliability.  
* Explain the social and ethical implications of their design decisions and how technology can support positive impact.
